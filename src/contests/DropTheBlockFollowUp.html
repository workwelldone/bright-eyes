Drop The Block Follow Up

Initial Thoughts

This was a fun game! It was fun having such an interesting problem to solve, playing against other players, and gaining valuable practice.

Evaluation of overall solution algorithm

I think the overall algorithm worked very well! I went into the solution without knowing for sure how much the computer could handle. It was a brute force solution, but with maximum flexibility and potential. It turned out that considering all possibilities of 5 blocks in advance was too much in 100 ms, but considering 4 blocks was optimal.

I wanted to further test and refine algorithm score results, but I ran out of time. After reaching the gold level, I learned how the scoring worked. The algorithm could have easily incorporate scoring weights, which could have further improved performance.

Conclusion
